# Troubleshooting

## No Image

- Check that a minimum resistance is present
- Verify wiring integrity
- Ensure CRT is powered correctly

---

## Image Distortion

- Expected when adjusting potentiometers
- Fine tuning is required

---

## Vertical Collapse

- Incorrect wiring of R350 replacement
- Potentiometer value too low

---

## Audio System Not Working

Possible causes:
- Insufficient amplification
- Improper signal injection point
- Comparator threshold not adapted
- Noise dominating signal

---

## Safety Issues

If abnormal behavior occurs:
- Power off immediately
- Discharge CRT before inspection
