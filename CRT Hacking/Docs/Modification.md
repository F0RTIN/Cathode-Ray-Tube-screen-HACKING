# Hardware Modifications

## RGB Channels

Each RGB channel was modified by replacing the original resistor with a potentiometer.

Configuration:
- 10kΩ potentiometer
- 1kΩ resistor in parallel

Purpose:
- Provide manual control over intensity
- Maintain a minimum resistance to protect the circuit

---

## Vertical Deflection

Resistor R350 was replaced by a 10kΩ potentiometer.

Effect:
- Alters the amplitude of the vertical deflection signal
- Allows real-time control of image height

---

## General Notes

- Modifications are invasive and depend on CRT model
- Careful calibration is required
- Incorrect values may damage the display
